Humidity

The humidity chart is an interesting one. It looks quite scattered but an obvious trend is that the humidity percentage is high in general. Regardless of where you are on the latitude scale you will face humitidity. We can also note towards 0 latitude there is much more dots to indicate more cities that face humidity. Which is true in the real world. Cities closer to the equator are more humid than cities north and south of the equator.
